About me

I am based in Twickenham and wanted a straightforward personal website that feels clean, readable, and easy to maintain. This page is designed as a simple starting point that can later grow into a fuller personal profile, portfolio, or contact site if needed.

A part of my life that matters to me is supporting people directly. I tend to back individuals directly by support of people's plans and try to give them a better chance of where they want to go. I also make much of the software and tooling I design available for others to use by open-sourcing a large part of what I build and releasing it under open-source and copyleft licences, including licences such as MIT and GPLv2.
